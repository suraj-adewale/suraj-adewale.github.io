Recognizing the paramount importance of catering to the multifaceted requirements of Nigerian youth, we firmly assert that this endeavor stands as a pivotal catalyst for the economic prosperity of Nigeria.

At the core of our youth empowerment approach lies a commitment to equipping young individuals with the necessary tools to thrive in the contemporary work environment, seize promising business prospects, and cultivate their entrepreneurial acumen.

Our aspiration is to unleash the untapped potential residing within the Nigerian youth population.

Our mission is to empower the future leaders of Nigeria by providing them with the essential skills and knowledge required for effective leadership, while also fostering a society that is proficient in information and communication technology (ICT).

With unwavering commitment, our focus lies in preparing the Nigerian youth for future leadership roles by igniting within them the drive to effect positive change, not only in their own lives but also within their communities.

about Malik Kuforijin Scholarship Foundation

At the heart of our mission is a steadfast dedication to investing in the boundless and untapped potential of Nigerian youth, recognizing it as the cornerstone for socioeconomic advancement, improved living standards, and heightened human efficiency within our society. With an unwavering vision, we aim to equip the future of our nation through the paramount avenues of education and information and communication technology (ICT). Our long-term goal is to bridge the knowledge gap, enabling our youth to compete in the global economy, while advocating for the integration of ICT into the Nigerian education policy as a fourth science, ensuring a progressive and continuous learning experience for all.
