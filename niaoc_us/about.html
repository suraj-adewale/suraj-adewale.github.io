About us

We deliver on a seamless travel experience!

Dominion and Shalom LLC World Travels specializes in student tours and over the past 15 years has arranged for hundreds of students to travel to a variety of destinations throughout the United States, UK, Australia, etc. with operations both in Nigeria and the US, we have a proven ability to deliver service solutions on a truly international scale.

At Dominion and Shalom LLC World Travels, whatever resourcing issues or technical challenges you may face, we can provide not just a solution, BUT a high-quality cost-effective solution.

Our purpose in providing certain services through joint venture companies is to access additional resources, expertise or local knowledge to the benefit of our clients on a pragmatic and economically effective basis.

All our joint venture partners are respected leaders in their own fields, with established track records and skill sets that specifically compliment our own.

Part of the benefits of working with a company like Dominion and Shalom LLC World Travels is that we help to ease the stress of documentation by providing the needed support by helping to plan and organize your travel itinerary for a guaranteed experience of maximum comfort and satisfaction.

 

At Dominion and Shalom LLC World Travels

    We exist to provide the necessary skills and resources to help our clients meet both challenges and opportunities in the Educational and travelling marketplace. We bring together a network of highly experienced practitioners with the knowledge and skills to deliver on a comprehensive range of high-quality services. We have successfully handled a wide range of projects over recent years – securing very high client satisfaction levels and a consistent reputation for capability and integrity. Committed to excellence in everything we do, we deliver tangible added value to our clients, focusing on quality and value for money.
